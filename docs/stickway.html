The Stickway feature set, corresponds with standard names and behavior.

[locl] Localized Forms
Substitutes character with the preferred form based on script language.
[frac] Fractions
Converts figures separated by slash with diagonal fraction.
[numr] Numerator
Converts to appropriate fraction numerator form, invoked by [frac].
[dnom] Denominator
Converts to appropriate fraction denominator form, invoked by [frac].
[sups] Superscript
Replaces character with superscript version, cf. [dnom]
[sinf] Scientific Inferiors
as in “H2O”, “SOx” or “YCbCr”.
[onum] Oldstyle Figures
Replaces numerals with cased old-style numerals, often also [pnum].
[lnum] Lining Figures
Replaces numerals with glyphs meant to fit better in all-capitals text, often also [tnum].
[case] Case Sensitive Forms
Replace characters, especially punctuation, with forms better suited for all-capital text, cf. [titl]
[smcp] Small Caps
Substitutes lower-case letters with small caps versions.
[c2sc] Capitals to Small Caps
Substitutes capital letters with small caps.
[tnum] Tabular Figures
Replaces numerals with glyphs of uniform width, often also [lnum].
[pnum] Proportional Figures
Replaces numerals with glyphs of proportional width, often also [onum].
[liga] Standard Ligatures
Replaces (by default) sequence of characters with a single ligature glyph.
[ss08] Stylistic Set #08
Range 1-20. Replaces character with one from a font-specific set of stylistic alternatives
[ss10] Stylistic Set #10
Range 1-20. Replaces character with one from a font-specific set of stylistic alternatives
[zero] Slashed Zero
Replaces 0 figure with slashed 0.

Supported weights

Weight values are respectively OS/2 and H-stem width.

  • Hairline (260, 16)
  • Thin (270, 22)
  • Light (300, 32)
  • Book (390, 52)
  • Regular (400, 72)
  • Semibold (600, 112)
  • Bold (700, 142)
  • Black (800, 182)

All weights are available as Roman and Italic.

Supported axes as Variable Font

  • wght (16, 90, 256) Weight Hairline:16, Light:32, Regular:90 Bold:150 Black:256
  • wdth (70, 100, 130) Width in percentage to Regular
  • CATL (0, 0, 500) Catalog of incompatible designs
  • opsz (8, 24, 24) Optical size, optimized for small sizes
  • RNDS (0, 0, 48) Maximum size of rounding corners for Regular. Serif roundings are smaller, limited to half of any stem.
  • GRAD (-8, 0, 8) Relative grade correction of stems

Catalog selectors

  • SANS (0-99) Sans
  • mono (100-199) Sans mono spaced
  • SLAB (200-299) Slab serif
  • FLRS (300-399) Sans with expanding flourishes
  • LAMP (400-499) Cabaret-like lamps
  • SCRP (500-600) Script (italic) with connecting latin glyphs.
