Supported weights

Weight values are respectively OS/2 and H-stem width.

  • Thin (270, 22)
  • Light (300, 32)
  • Book (390, 52)
  • Regular (400, 72)
  • Semibold (600, 112)
  • Bold (700, 142)
  • Black (800, 182)

All weights are available as Roman and Italic.
